\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Vigenre Cipher -\/ Polyalphabetic substitution. 

Uses a keyword to create multiple \doxylink{classfilevault_1_1algorithms_1_1classical_1_1Caesar}{Caesar} shifts. Considered "{}le chiffre indchiffrable"{} (unbreakable cipher) until 1863.

Security\+: BROKEN -\/ Vulnerable to Kasiski examination Purpose\+: Educational -\/ shows improvement over monoalphabetic ciphers
