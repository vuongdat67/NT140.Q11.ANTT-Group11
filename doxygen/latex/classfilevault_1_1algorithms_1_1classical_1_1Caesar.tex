\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{classfilevault_1_1algorithms_1_1classical_1_1Caesar}{Caesar} Cipher -\/ Educational only. 

Simple substitution cipher that shifts characters by a fixed offset. Used by Julius \doxylink{classfilevault_1_1algorithms_1_1classical_1_1Caesar}{Caesar} for military messages (\texorpdfstring{$\sim$}{\string~}50 BC).

Security\+: COMPLETELY BROKEN -\/ Only 26 possible keys! Purpose\+: Educational demonstration of cryptanalysis
