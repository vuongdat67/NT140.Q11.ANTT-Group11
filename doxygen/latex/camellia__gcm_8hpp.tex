\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Camellia-\/\+GCM AEAD encryption algorithm. 

Camellia is a Japanese symmetric key block cipher developed by Mitsubishi Electric and NTT. It\textquotesingle{}s approved by ISO/\+IEC, CRYPTREC, and NESSIE. It has similar security level to AES.
